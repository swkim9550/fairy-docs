Privacy Policy

Fairy prioritizes your privacy above all else

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

1. Overview

Fairy (hereinafter "App") prioritizes the protection of user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information.

ğŸ”’ Core Principle All diary entries and personal data are stored only on your device. Nothing is transmitted to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Local Storage Data

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Diary entries and emotion records
  • Fairy character selection and settings
  • Quest progress
  • AI-generated images (Fairy Gallery)
  • Time capsule data
  • App settings and themes

This data is never transmitted to external servers.

2.2 Fairy Gallery (Optional)

Only when you voluntarily exhibit artwork:

  • Exhibited images (stored on Supabase cloud)
  • Anonymous nickname
  • Like counts

No personally identifiable information is stored.

2.3 Analytics Data

We collect the following anonymized data for app improvement:

  • App usage statistics (sessions, active users)
  • Feature usage patterns
  • App crash information
  • Device information (OS version, device model)

3. How We Use Information

  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs
  • Enhancing user experience
  • Prioritizing feature development

4. Data Protection

  • All diary and personal data stored only on your device
  • All local data deleted when app is uninstalled
  • iCloud backup supported (user's choice)

5. User Rights

  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Request deletion of exhibited artwork
  • Contact us regarding data processing

6. Contact Us

For inquiries about data processing:

  • Email: kbkkim2@naver.com
  • Instagram: @fairy.daybook

7. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated without notice. Significant changes will be announced within the App.
